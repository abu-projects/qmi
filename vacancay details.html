The Executive Secretary is responsible for providing high-level administrative support to the executive team, managing schedules, coordinating meetings, and handling confidential information with discretion.

  • Manage executive's calendar, schedule meetings, and coordinate travel arrangements
  • Prepare and edit correspondence, presentations, and reports
  • Handle confidential information with discretion
  • Coordinate and attend meetings, take minutes, and follow up on action items
  • Act as a liaison between executives and internal/external stakeholders
  • Manage office supplies and maintain filing systems

This position is based at our headquarters in Doha, Qatar. The work environment is professional, fast-paced, and collaborative.

Working hours are Sunday to Thursday, 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, with occasional flexibility required for special events or urgent matters.

  • Bachelor's degree in Business Administration or related field
  • Minimum 3 years of experience as an executive assistant or similar role
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook)
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English and Arabic
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Exceptional attention to detail and accuracy
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality and exercise discretion
  • Strong interpersonal skills and professional demeanor
  • Ability to prioritize tasks and work under pressure
  • Problem-solving skills and proactive approach
  • Cultural sensitivity and adaptability
